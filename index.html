Consumer Price Index vs. Political Affiliation of Presidents Starting in 1981

Hypothesis: Consumer Price Index will be higher when a democratic president is in power.

Conclusion: The data seems to show that CPI tends to increase more when Republicans are in control of the White House

There are a lot of things to consider when looking at CPI as not all presidents enforce the same policies even within the same party.
This is why I choose to show both president CPI term averages with a histogram and a blanket scatter plot of CPI percent increases year by year
with color indicating party.
